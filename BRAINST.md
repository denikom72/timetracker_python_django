Vision Statement / BRAINSTORMING

Create an intuitive, secure, and automated system that simplifies customers or employee attendance tracking using QR codes and role-based access, reducing manual errors and improving accountability.

- High-Level Goals

Automate attendance tracking — remove need for manual entry or paper logs.

Provide transparency — management can view attendance in real time.

Ensure accessibility — simple, mobile-friendly QR code system for all employees.

Enable analytics — export attendance data for payroll and compliance.

Offer scalability — support future features like leave management and geolocation check-ins.
